Welcome to my Hue Two: A Duotone Zoo web app! Duotone is a cool design effect that adds two contrasting colors to an image, giving it a unique, artistic vibe. It works by layering one halftone color over another, usually to highlight the midtones and highlights in a way that stands out.

To get started, search Unsplash for an image (or upload your own image), pick two colors you like, and click "Apply Effect" to see how it transforms your image. If you like the result, be sure to hit that Download button!
